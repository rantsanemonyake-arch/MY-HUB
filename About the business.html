ABOUT OUR BUSINESS

Farmstead is an agricultural enterprise established to cultivate and harvest seasonal vegetables across Lesotho`s fertile valleys. The business aims to respond to increaing local demand for fresh, affordable and high-quality vegetables while also supplying large-scale buyers such as supermarkets schools and hotels. The enterprise will focus on producing vegetables in line with seasonal patterns to ensure freshness, cost reduction and maintaining consistent supply throughout the year. Farmstead further aims to provide a consistent and reliable source of healthy abd fresh vegetables thoughout all the corners of Lesotho.

The business seeks financial and technical support to enhance production capacity, adopt morden irrigation techniques and as well promote sustainable farming practices. By creating job opportunities and supporting food security, Farmsteads will contribute to Lesothos agricultural and economic development.The main focus of this enterprise is to encourage the farmers in Lesotho to make use of the fertile valleys of Lesotho regardless of the season of the year. Also, due to the high rate of imported vegetables in the country, this enterprise seeks to minimize the rate of imports from other countries.
