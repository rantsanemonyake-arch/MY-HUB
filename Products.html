OUR PRODUCTS

Our products are usually classified into seasons, whereby each season has its kind of vegetables to produce. This is to ensure that the business has a sustainability in grops harvesting and an endless flow of vegetables distribution to the Basotho Nation.

  1. SUMMER: (Tomatoes, Green beans and Peppers)
  2. AUTUMN: (Carrots, Cabbage and Lettuce)
  3. WINTER: (Spinach and Kale)
  4. SPRING: (Maize, Wheat and Barley)
